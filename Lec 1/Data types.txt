 Primitive : 
1, byte
Size: 1 byte
Range: -128 to 127
Use: small integers, saving memory.

2,short
Size: 2 bytes
Range: -32,768 to 32,767

3,int (most used)
Size: 4 bytes
Range: -2,147,483,648 to 2,147,483,647
Default for most counting operations.

4,long
Size: 8 bytes
Use: big numbers
Example: 12345678900L (add L)

5, float
Size: 4 bytes
Decimal number with less precision
Example: 3.14f (add f)
